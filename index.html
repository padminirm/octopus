The Navodaya Trust

Our Vision: An India, that Promotes equality for all, across the divides of gender, caste, religion and region. Ensures and promotes human dignity, justice and freedom of thought, expression, identity and action. Seeks to maintain peace and amity for all, within the country and across the world. Our Mission: Navodaya supports social and ecological justice, and equality for all, across the gender and economic spectrum. Navodaya opposes discriminatory economic, patriarchal and misogynistic forces.
